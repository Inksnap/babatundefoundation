OUR VALUES

Advocacy and Policy Reform

In addition to direct interventions, WBEF actively engages in advocacy and policy reform activities to address systemic issues that perpetuate inequality and marginalization. We will collaborate with government bodies, international organizations, and community leaders to promote inclusive policies and create a conducive environment for the less privileged.

Read More

Healthcare Access

Recognizing the importance of health in overall well-being, WBEF strives to improve healthcare access for the less privileged. We are seting up medical clinics, organize health camps, and collaborate with medical professionals to provide essential medical services, vaccinations, and health education.

Read More

Education for all

WBEF firmly believes that education is a fundamental right that should be accessible to all individuals, irrespective of their socioeconomic status. We work tirelessly to ensure that children from disadvantaged backgrounds receive quality education by providing scholarships, and offering educational resources and support alongside Vocational skills.

Read More

Skill Development and Employment

WBEF understands that equipping individuals with the necessary skills and training is vital for their economic independence. We offer vocational training programs, entrepreneurship development initiatives, and job placement assistance, enabling the less privileged to secure sustainable livelihoods and uplift their families.

Read More

Emergency Relief

During times of crises, such as natural disasters or conflicts, WBEF will swiftly respond to provide immediate relief to affected communities. We offer emergency shelter, food, clean water, and medical aid, ensuring the safety and well-being of those in distress.

Read More

Partnerships & Volunteering

WBEF recognizes the power of collaboration and actively seeks partnerships with like-minded organizations, businesses, and individuals who shares our vision. We also offer volunteering opportunities for individuals who want to contribute their time and skills towards the cause, amplifying their impact and creating a sense of collective responsibility.

Read More

Every life counts

WBEF is actively making a difference by providing support, empowerment, and hope to those in need.

FAQ

How can I make a donation?

You can make a donation by clicking the "Donate Now" button at the top of the page or visiting our donation page. We accept various payment methods for your convenience.

How do I become a volunteer?

To become a volunteer, please visit our Volunteer page and fill out the application form. Our team will contact you with the next steps.

Where does my donation go?

Your donation directly supports our programs, including healthcare, education, emergency relief, and community development. We are committed to transparency and maximizing the impact of every contribution.

How can I get updates about your work?

Subscribe to our newsletter at the bottom of this page or follow us on social media to receive the latest news, stories, and updates about our impact and upcoming events.
